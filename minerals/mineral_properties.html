Physical Properties of Minerals

There are approximately 4000 different minerals, and each of those minerals has a unique set of physical properties. These include:

  • color
  • streak
  • hardness
  • luster
  • diaphaneity
  • specific gravity
  • cleavage
  • fracture
  • magnetism
  • solubility
  • and many more

These physical properties are useful for identifying minerals. However, they are much more important in determining the potential industrial uses of the mineral.
